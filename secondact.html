COTTON.

Humph! Grounds! For a ring! Aye, there'll be no diabolical circle here for the devil to daunce in. I will question Betty thereon.

[_Rises._]

Do thou remain here and I will send her to thee. Oh, that he should offer daughter of mine a ring!


ADONIJAH.

[_Frostily._]

Good-afternoon, Sir Charles, mine host is absent.


CHARLES.

[_Stepping in._]

My mission has rather to do with Mistress Betty. Is she in?
